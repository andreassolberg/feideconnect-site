UserIDs

How to identify users, people and accounts within Connect.

Primary UserID

...

The primary user identifier is generated for users the first time they access Connect. It is in the UUID format, and is opaque, not releasing any additional information about the user. It is intended to be long-term, surviving merging of schools and universities.
